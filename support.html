Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a show?
Tap the search icon on the Now Showing tab, search for any TV show or movie, and tap to add it to your library.
How do I remove a show?
Open the show's detail page, scroll down, and tap "Remove from Library." You can also tap a tracked show in the provider catalog to untrack it.
Where is my data stored?
All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't have accounts or cloud storage, so your data is completely private.
Will I lose my data if I delete the app?
Yes. Since all data is stored on your device, deleting the app removes your tracked shows and preferences. We recommend keeping the app installed.
Why is a show missing or showing wrong information?
Show and movie data comes from TMDB and TVMaze. If something looks wrong, it's likely an issue with those databases. You can help by contributing corrections at themoviedb.org.
How do notifications work?
When enabled, the app checks for new episodes of your tracked shows and sends you a notification when new content is available.
